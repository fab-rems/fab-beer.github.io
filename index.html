100K CAN GOAL

The Baby Dictator Project (BDP) is to celebrate the beginning good of all human beings!

Selling 100k cans, we will invest the profits from can sales to replace the fallen baby balloon one thousand times over! We will create artistic inflatables ready to roll out in every major city on the Trump 2020 rally-route! Recruiting rally supporters across the country, we will celebrate the innocence of young life in the form of positive, meaningful inflatables lent out for free to fans and floated over rallies, nationwide.
